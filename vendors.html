Wedding Dress - Sally C Designs

My wedding dress was a mixture from photos of dresses from the internet and photos and designs found in New Zealand.  It was an 1886 design victorian wedding dress for many reasons - I loved the bustle era, earlier years were too ribbony and frilly for my liking and later years were coming back into natural form dresses.

Sally was moving from the USA to England at the time she was making and helping me design my dress.  Even through her upheaval, kept in contact and made sure that she understood what I wanted and made the distance seem not even a bit of a hurdle.

It was the most perfect wedding dress I could have hoped for.

http://www.sallycdesigns.com
